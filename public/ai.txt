  - PromptPay is the standard digital payment method
  - Buddhist holidays and Royal ceremonies affect availability
  - "Wai" greeting, respect for hierarchy important in business
  - Thai language support critical for local market

Booking-Process:
  1. Customer browses artist profiles
  2. Customer requests quote via form or LINE
  3. Artist provides custom quote
  4. Customer books and pays deposit (50%)
  5. Event happens
  6. Remaining payment (50%)
  7. Customer leaves review

Payment-Methods:
  - PromptPay (QR code, Thai standard)
  - Bank Transfer
